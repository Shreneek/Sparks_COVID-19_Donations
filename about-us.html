SPARKS COVID-19 DONATIONS

Since March 2020, Sparks COVID-19 Donations Team has provided immediate relief assistance to over 2.3 lakh poor peoples by distributing 79,855 dry ration kits and supported hospitals, frontline workers with 18,992 PPE kits, 5,22,107 masks, 50,706 litres of sanitisers and 2,000 litres of disinfectant.

Sparks COVID-19 Donations aims to reach over 100 million people belonging to poor communities by 2030 and help them overcome poverty.

So far, we have been able to directly impact the lives of over 1 million children and families.
